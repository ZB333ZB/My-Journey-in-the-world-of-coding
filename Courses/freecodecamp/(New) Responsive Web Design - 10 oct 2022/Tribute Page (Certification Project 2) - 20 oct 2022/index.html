Babymetal

A Japanese kawaii metal music band

Here's a brief time line of Babymetal's history:

  • 2009 - Kobametal, the band's producer and long-time heavy metal enthusiast, started planning the launch of an idol/heavy metal fusion group after he was impressed by Suzuka Nakamoto's performance with her previous group Karen Girl's, thinking she would be suitable as the lead vocalist for the new group.
  • 2010 - The band was formed in Tokyo, Japan, as the heavy music club (重音部, Jūonbu), or sub-unit, of the female idol group Sakura Gakuin.
  • November 28, 2010 - Babymetal's first live appearance at Sakura Gakuin's first solo concert, Sakura Gakuin Festival ☆ 2010.
  • July 2011 - Babymetal premiered the song, Ijime, Dame, Zettai (No More Bullying), at a Sakura Gakuin concert
  • April 27, 2011 - The band's first single, Doki Doki Morning, released
  • March 2012 - Babymetal's first CD single, a collaboration with the band Kiba of Akiba, titled Babymetal / Kiba of Akiba, released in March 2012 by Juonbu Records.
  • August 2012 - Babymetal debuted at Japan's Summer Sonic Festival, becoming the youngest act ever to perform at the festival, at the average age of 12.
  • November 2012 - Babymetal performed outside of Japan for the first time at the 2012 Anime Festival Asia in Singapore.
  • March 1 and 2, 2014 - The band gave two concerts at Budokan. With the average age of 14.7, they became youngest-ever female act to perform a show there. The two concerts were attended by 20,000 people.
  • March 2, 2014 - Babymetal announced their first world tour, the Babymetal World Tour 2014.
  • July 27, 2014 - The band went on to make their debut appearance in the United States with a headlining show at The Fonda Theatre in Hollywood.
  • August 9, 2014 - They played Heavy Montréal 2014 in Canada alongside Metallica, Slayer and Anthrax
  • August 16, 2014 - Babymetal performed at Summer Sonic 2014 in Japan with bands such as Avenged Sevenfold and Megadeth.
  • April 1, 2016 - Babymetal's second album, Metal Resistance, released
  • April 5, 2016 - Babymetal made their U.S. television debut, performing Gimme Chocolate!! on The Late Show with Stephen Colbert.
  • September 19 and September 20, 2016 - Babymetal ended their Babymetal World Tour 2016: Legend Metal Resistance by performing at the Tokyo Dome, with both nights sold out and approximately 55,000 people attending each night.
  • October 19, 2018 - The band officially announced Yuimetal's departure from the group
